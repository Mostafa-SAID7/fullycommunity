# Product Overview

Fully Community is a modern full-stack community platform with AI-powered features for automotive enthusiasts. It provides a comprehensive ecosystem for car owners and enthusiasts to connect, share knowledge, and access services.

## Core Features

- **Community Features**: Posts, groups, events, Q&A forums, guides, maps, news, and reviews
- **Marketplace**: Products, orders, auctions, cart, and seller management
- **Services**: Car wash, customization, driving school, fuel/charging, garage rentals, inspection, insurance, maintenance, registration, repairs, and roadside assistance
- **Media**: Podcasts (shows, episodes, live recordings) and videos (channels, live streams)
- **AI Assistant**: HuggingFace-powered intelligent assistant for automated support
- **Authentication**: Secure JWT-based auth with ASP.NET Identity, 2FA, device tracking, and breach detection
- **Real-time**: SignalR hubs for chat and notifications

## Platform Support

- **Web**: Angular 19 SPA with admin and main portals
- **Mobile**: Flutter app for iOS and Android
- **API**: RESTful API with comprehensive Swagger documentation organized by feature domains
